How to Play

Game Rules

  • Choose your board size: 3√ó3, 4√ó4, or 5√ó5
  • Players take turns placing X or O
  • Get N in a row to win (3, 4, or 5 based on board size)
  • If all cells are filled with no winner, it's a draw

Board Sizes

  • 3√ó3: Classic mode - get 3 in a row
  • 4√ó4: Medium board - get 4 in a row
  • 5√ó5: Large board - get 5 in a row

Game Modes

  • Player vs Player: Play with a friend
  • Easy: AI makes random moves
  • Medium: AI plays smart sometimes
  • Hard: Unbeatable AI using Minimax

Tips

  • Control the center for an advantage
  • Watch for opponent's winning moves
  • Try to create two ways to win
